Document Request Portal

Request the Arizona A.R.S. 33-1260 resale disclosure package for any Scottsdale Park Place unit.

Realtor
Arizona A.R.S. 33-1260 requires the HOA to provide the resale disclosure package within 10 days of a written request. Submit your request below and the board will prepare and deliver all available documents. Items requiring manual preparation (lien estoppel, assessment status letter) will be flagged and completed by the property manager. The board may charge up to $400 per A.R.S. 33-1260(C) for package preparation.
