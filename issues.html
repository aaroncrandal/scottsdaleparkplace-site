Reserve Fund Status

The May 2024 reserve study found Scottsdale Park Place at 25% funded — significantly below the recommended threshold. The current annual contribution of $117,000 is insufficient to meet the $300,000+ recommended level. The board is reviewing the funding model with FDReserve Studies, LLC (William Schlimgen, PE) in March 2026. A proposed strategy will be shared with owners following that review.

About This Timeline

All project data is sourced from the FDReserve Studies reserve study update dated May 25, 2024. Scheduled years and cost estimates are from that study and will shift based on available funds, vendor bids, and board decisions. The full study is available in the Documents section. Questions can be directed to the board via the Contact page.
